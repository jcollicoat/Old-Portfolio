Produse App




< brief >

Design and develop a mobile app targeted at saving water and combatting the drought in California and elsewhere.


< concept >

My Produse app was designed to help users track their water consumption while purchasing supermarket goods. Through scanning barcodes of goods a user is buying the app will track water consumption on a shopping list and monthly scale, as well as suggesting more eco-friendly alternative products. The concept behind the branding comes from the water molecule, H2O (two hydrogen atoms circling an oxygen).
