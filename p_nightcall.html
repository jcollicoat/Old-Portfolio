Nightcall




< brief >

Produce a series of photographs representative of a song or piece of music.


< concept >

For this project I chose to depict the song "Nightcall" by Kavinsky, an electronic song which is most well-known for its use in the opening credits sequence of the film "Drive". I wanted to capture the orange-blue colour scale which is used heavily in "Drive", as well as capturing similar shots to the film while acting out the song's lyrics.
