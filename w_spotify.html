Spotify Desktop Re-design




< brief >

Self-initiated project to re-design the Spotify desktop application.


< concept >

Alongside improving the user experience of the desktop app, I wanted to make the visuals more interesting. I added a lot more imagery to the pages in the application, including integrating background images behind content. Functionally, I made improvements to the Saved Music section, as well as overhauling the Browse and Artist pages to be more efficient in displaying content. Finally, I improved the control bar in order to save display space.
