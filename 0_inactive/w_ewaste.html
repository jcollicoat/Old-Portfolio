E-Waste



< brief >

Produce a video promoting awareness on a social environmental issue in New Zealand, and create an accompanying website.


< concept >

For this project we chose to promote the issue of excessive electronic waste. The video included statistics and interviews laid over footage from electronics stores as well as recycling and garbage centers. The message promoted was that it is important but simple to make a difference by recycling electronic devices such as phones, computers and televisions. The accompanying website featured a town which cycled from day to night, and a recycling truck drove down the street occasionally to support the message.
