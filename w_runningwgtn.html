Running WGTN




< brief >

Create a small booklet to promote running to New Zealand tourists.


< concept >

Running shoes serve as the basis for illustrative representations of running locations around Wellington (WGTN), New Zealand. Iconography is used to quickly detail the difficulty and length of each course. A height map is also included as a small visual element on each spread.
