Jack London's "The Call of the Wild"




< brief >

Design and produce a hand-bound, hard-cover book which creates a renewed interest in a classic banned book.


< concept >

The theme of primitivism in The Call of the Wild is important, and I wanted to use illustrations suggestive of cave-drawings to represent this theme. Black and red are used in my illustrations as a result, although the red is also used as blood, due to the common violence in the story. Further, the illustrations are located the the beginnings of each chapter and represent a key scene or idea found in that chapter.
