Quasar Saxophone Quartet




< brief >

Design a promotional poster for the Quasar Saxophone Quartet.


< concept >

This project was entirely type-based, therefore it was essential to create an interesting composition - creating visual hierarchy through size, colour and positioning - without the use of images or illustrations. As this project was a promotional piece, it was also important to make sure that while I included all the required content, the most important information (e.g. place, date and who) was initially striking to the viewer, even from a distance.
