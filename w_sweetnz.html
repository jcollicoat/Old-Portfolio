Sweet New Zealand




< brief >

Design a cookbook containing recipes pertaining to a common theme.


< concept >

I chose "sweet" recipes which originated in New Zealand as the subject for my cookbook. Further, the recipes featured in Sweet New Zealand are all based off Collicoat family recipes, adding a personal flavour to the book. Stylistically, I used "hand-script" typefaces as well as adding my own little symbols to generate the laid-back attitude of New Zealand. The black-through-grey colour scheme is also representative of New Zealand's colours.
