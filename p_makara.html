Lens: Makara




< brief >

Produce a series of photographs depicting a significant place.


< concept >

Makara beach is significant to me as I went there many times with my family as a child. The conditions at the beach are often harsh and cold, and I wanted to represent this through the photographs, using film grain and black-and-white. I also focused on the rock textures found at Makara. The tin-foil is representative of cooking - often we would cook dinner over on open fire on the rocky shores.
